Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Happy Heritage Kitchen

How do I place an order?
Placing an order is simple! Browse our menu, select your favorite dishes, and add them to your cart. Then proceed to checkout where you can provide your delivery details and choose a payment method. The entire process takes just a few minutes!
What are your delivery hours?
We deliver from Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and on Sundays from 11:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Our delivery team ensures your food arrives fresh and at the perfect temperature within 30-45 minutes of placing your order.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely! We offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. When placing your order, you can specify any dietary restrictions in the delivery notes, and our chefs will prepare your meal with the utmost care to meet your needs.
How far in advance should I place a large order?
For orders serving more than 10 people, we recommend placing your order at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to ensure we have all the necessary ingredients and can allocate the proper preparation time for your special event.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery. All transactions are secure and encrypted. For corporate accounts, we also offer monthly billing options.
Can I schedule a recurring delivery?
Yes! We offer a subscription service where you can schedule regular deliveries on a weekly or bi-weekly basis. Subscribers enjoy a 10% discount and priority preparation. Contact us to set up your personalized meal plan.
